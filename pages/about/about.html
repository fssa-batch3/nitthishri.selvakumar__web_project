Modern technology

Blackwoodalley Restorations has been providing quality antique electronics and furniture restorations for client. Our expertise coupled with modern technology allows us to do what most repair facilities cannot. While our goal is to keep all of the pieces we work on as original as possible, sometimes the parts are just not available anymore. We pair modern technology such as: 3D Printing, Laser Cutting, Molding and CAD Design to re- create, in house, parts we need. Our technicians are trained to repair all brands of vintage radio from the early 1900’s to the late 1970’s, from very rare and priceless antique units to the popular and more sentimental units.
