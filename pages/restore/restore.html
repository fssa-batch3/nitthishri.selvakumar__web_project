At Blackwood alley Restoration, our highest priority is providing authentic antique restoration—producing results that stand the test of time and protect the longevity of your piece. As such, we do not take shortcuts, as is often the case with what we refer to as, “refinishers”. We are professional restorers that antique furniture and electronics as a part of the holistic restoration process.

How do you know which approach is best for your piece(s)? There are differences in each approach- the age and condition of the peice, your goals and your budget will determine which one is best for your piece(s).The restoration COnsultants at Blackwood alley can talk with you about your piece, provide a physical evaluation, if it makes senses and guide you through the best option to meet your goals and vision.
